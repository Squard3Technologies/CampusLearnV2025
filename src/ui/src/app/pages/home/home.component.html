Welcome to Campus Learn

CampusLearn is an e-learning platform designed to provide additional learning resources and academic support to students at Belgium Campus who need extra help in certain IT related topics.

The platform allows students to:
  • Create topics for which they need help
  • Access learning materials uploaded by tutors
  • Receive feedback on their progress

CampusLearn supports multimedia content such as videos, documents, and interactive lessons, and integrates a messaging API to facilitate communication between students and registered peer tutors.

Our goal is to enhance the academic experience by offering flexible, accessible learning opportunities outside of the traditional classroom setting.
